## Listing Description

Turnipp: The Values-Driven Marketplace for Modern Dining

Turnipp.com has established itself as a prominent player in the online marketplace sector, specializing in the Food and Drink industry. Founded in 2025, this innovative platform has carved out a niche by providing a curated guide that spotlights independent venues committed to sustainability, fairness, and real community impact. Rather than focusing on trending popularity or corporate giants, Turnipp champions the "change makers"—those who prioritize purpose over stars.

? Operations: A Mission-Aligned Marketplace

Turnipp operates primarily through a **subscription-based and performance-driven business model**, offering specialized packages tailored to the needs of sustainable restaurants. This approach allows venues to keep their focus on their mission while Turnipp handles the connection to conscious consumers.

- **Diverse Service Tiers**: Turnipp provides three distinct paths for venues to join their network:
	- **Walk-ins (Basic)**: Focuses on getting listed and discovered for a low **£10 monthly subscription**.
		- **Pro**: Offers enhanced visibility, including social media promotion and curated content, for a **£20 monthly subscription**.
		- **Turnipp 10**: A flagship performance-based model where venues pay a **£2 flat fee per person booked**, receiving the "Top Sustainable Restaurant Badge" and premium perks.
- **Onboarding & Community**: To maintain platform integrity, every listing is handpicked to avoid "greenwashing". Restaurants complete a quick onboarding form to share their specific story—whether it's farm-to-table sourcing, zero-waste kitchens, or inclusive hiring.
- **Market Penetration**: Turnipp expands its reach by positioning itself as the bridge between ethical producers and "conscious diners" who choose where to eat based on shared values rather than just reviews.

? Customers: The Conscious Dining Ecosystem

The marketplace caters to a growing audience of **ethically-minded food lovers** seeking authentic dining experiences. Turnipp has created an engaging ecosystem that values **traceability and transparency**.

- **Verified Visibility**: Buyers can find venues through curated city guides, interactive maps, and editorial features that highlight specific sustainable practices.
- **Value-Based Selection**: Customers use Turnipp's **6 Categories** to find restaurants that meet their personal ethics:
	1. Farm to Table/Local Product
		2. Plant-Based/Vegan Options
		3. Renewable Energy
		4. Zero Waste
		5. Fair Trade Ingredients
		6. Impactful Visibility (Local Gems)

?️ Technology: Delivering Seamless Traceability

Leveraging sophisticated technological solutions, Turnipp employs a suite of tools to ensure every venue's mission is visible and every transaction is secure.

- **Insights & Performance**: Google Analytics plays a pivotal role in understanding user behavior and site performance, offering insights that help restaurants understand what "conscious diners" are looking for.
- **Financial Infrastructure**: To facilitate secure financial transactions, the company utilizes **Stripe and Revolut**. These platforms ensure seamless payment processing for the subscription and booking-fee models.
- **Management Integration**: For financial management, **Xero** provides reliable accounting solutions that streamline reporting as the platform scales its partner network.

? Financials: Growth Grounded in Sustainability

Since its inception in 2025, Turnipp.com has experienced consistent growth, driven by a revenue model that favors **transparency over commission**.

- **Introductory Incentives**: To drive platform adoption, Turnipp often offers its packages **free for the first 3 months**, allowing venues to experience the impact before committing financially.
- **Predictability vs. Performance**: By offering both fixed monthly subscriptions (£10–£20) and a performance-based booking fee (£2 per person), Turnipp creates a robust financial foundation while remaining accessible to restaurants of all sizes.
- **Strategic Growth**: The stability afforded by recurring subscription revenues allows Turnipp to invest in editorial content and community features, positioning it as a long-term guide in a competitive landscape.
