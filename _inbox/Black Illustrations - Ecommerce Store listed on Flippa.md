## Listing Description

**BlackIllustrations.com — Established Niche Illustration Brand with 140K Email List**

Founded in 2021, BlackIllustrations.com has built one of the most recognized illustration brands serving a culturally significant, structurally underserved market: high-quality illustrations depicting Black and African-diaspora characters in everyday and professional contexts.

**The numbers tell the story.**

The business has generated over $389,000 in lifetime revenue across 94,500+ Gumroad transactions. The asset library has grown to approximately 5,500 individual illustrations across 98 distinct packs and covering professional, educational, lifestyle, holiday, and themed categories. Every illustration is owned outright by the business. There are zero royalty splits, zero licensing complications, zero ongoing payment obligations to original artists. This is a clean, fully-transferable IP asset.

**The audience is the moat.**

The brand has built an engaged email list of approximately 140,000 subscribers — designers, agencies, educators, nonprofit marketers, and brand teams who actively use illustrations in their work. This list has been significantly under-monetized in recent months. Even a modest reactivation campaign with a 0.5% conversion to a $20 product would generate $14,000 from a single send. The list is the single most valuable asset in this acquisition.

Beyond email, the brand has built presence across Instagram (5,560 followers, 964 posts), Facebook (7,100 followers), and YouTube (434 subscribers, 35 videos). All accounts transfer with the sale.

**Traffic and SEO.**

Google Analytics shows 175,000+ active users with strong organic search performance on category-defining keywords. The top-ranking page ("Illustrations of Black People") alone has generated 73,000+ views. Traffic comes primarily from Direct (95K sessions) and Organic Search (71K), with the United States as the dominant market followed by China, India, the UK, Singapore, Canada, and Brazil. The traffic profile is high-quality, intent-driven, and entirely organic — zero paid acquisition spend.

**Brand credibility.**

BlackIllustrations.com has been recognized through partnership with Ally Bank, a meaningful enterprise-level validation of the brand's reach and quality. This signals real B2B licensing potential that has not been formally productized.

**Operations.**

The business currently runs on under 2 hours per week of owner involvement. Monthly expenses are approximately $150 — hosting, email platform, and basic tools. Revenue flows through Gumroad with clean reporting and easy buyer due diligence.

**Why I'm selling.**

I'm fully focused on my primary business. BlackIllustrations.com has been maintained passively rather than actively grown. The brand, library, and audience are too valuable to sit idle. The right buyer in a designer, illustrator, or creative entrepreneur with focus to dedicate can unlock significant growth opportunities the business has not yet pursued.

**Growth opportunities (clear paths the next owner can run):**

The email list is the lowest-hanging fruit — a focused broadcast cadence with new offers can immediately reactivate dormant subscribers. New illustration production has paused; resuming output (especially with AI-assisted tooling) would create regular launch moments and re-engage the audience. A subscription tier offering full library access for a monthly fee is a natural recurring-revenue layer absent from the current business. The Ally Bank partnership demonstrates enterprise interest, but no formal B2B licensing program exists yet companies regularly need illustration licenses at $500–$5,000+ tiers, and a dedicated landing page targeting that buyer would be straightforward to build. The YouTube channel has only 35 videos but the niche has very low YouTube competition. Adjacent visual categories (Latinx and Asian-diaspora illustration libraries) could be launched under the same operational playbook.

**Included in the sale.**

Domain (BlackIllustrations.com), full illustration library (~5,500 assets / 98 packs), Gumroad store with full transaction history, email list of ~140,000 subscribers, all social media accounts (Instagram, Facebook, YouTube), brand assets, and full operational documentation. 1–2 weeks of post-sale handover support included.

**Buyer requirements.**

Asset transfer via Escrow.com strongly preferred. Verified buyers receive full Gumroad analytics and Google Analytics access under NDA. Serious inquiries only.
