## Listing Description

**About the Business**

**MotorcycleInsurance.org.uk** is a UK focused insurance affiliate website dedicated to motorcycle insurance. The site attracts users actively searching for insurance information and quotes, and monetises this traffic through affiliate partnerships with insurance providers.

Launched in **2013**, the website benefits from a long established domain history, strong niche relevance, and trust within the UK motorcycle insurance space. It operates in a proven and competitive vertical, alongside comparison sites, insurers, and automotive publishers, but focuses purely on motorcycle insurance, allowing it to target high intent search traffic.

This sale includes the **website, domain, and existing affiliate setup**, making it a turnkey digital asset for a buyer looking for passive or semi passive income.

**Business Model**

The business operates on a **pure affiliate marketing model**.

Revenue is generated when users click through from the website to partner insurance providers and complete a quote or policy action. The site earns commission per successful referral.

There are:

- No products
- No fulfilment
- No staff
- No inventory
- No customer service

There are no long term contracts with affiliates, giving a buyer flexibility to:

- Add new insurance partners
- Change affiliate networks
- Introduce lead generation or comparison tools

Once set up, the site requires no active involvement to continue earning.

**Key Metrics**

Key metrics buyers should assess include:

- Long established domain (since 2013)
- Organic search traffic driven by insurance intent
- Consistent affiliate click through activity
- High value niche (insurance)
- Zero operating costs
- No admin workload
- Fully passive income structure
- UK targeted audience
- SEO driven traffic potential

Full access to analytics and affiliate performance data can be provided to serious buyers.

**Operations**

This business is **completely hands off**.

There are:

- No day to day tasks
- No customer enquiries
- No orders or fulfilment
- No staff or contractors
- No software development requirements

Once affiliate links are in place, the site runs automatically. Hosting and domain renewal are the only considerations, both of which are minimal.

This is a true **set and forget digital asset**.

**Customers**

The audience consists of **UK based motorcyclists** actively researching insurance options.

Visitors are typically:

- New riders
- Experienced bikers renewing policies
- Riders comparing quotes to reduce premiums

Users are usually one time visitors with high purchase intent, which suits the affiliate model well. There is also clear opportunity to increase lifetime value by adding:

- Email capture
- Renewal reminders
- Expanded content around bike ownership

**Financials**

The business operates with **no running costs**, meaning all affiliate revenue is effectively profit.

Revenue has been stable, supported by ongoing organic traffic and the evergreen nature of insurance search demand. Motorcycle insurance is a recurring requirement, making this a resilient niche.

Detailed affiliate earnings and performance history can be shared with qualified buyers.

**Growth Opportunities**

There is significant upside for a buyer with SEO or affiliate experience, including:

- Expanding content to target additional keywords
- Improving conversion rate optimisation
- Adding comparison tables or calculators
- Partnering with additional insurers
- Building an email list for renewals
- Scaling with paid search or display ads

A buyer with digital marketing or SEO skills could increase revenue substantially with minimal investment.

**Reason for Selling**

The owner is selling to **focus on other, larger operational businesses**. Rather than letting the site sit idle or liquidating it, the decision has been made to sell it to someone who can further develop or monetise it.

The **asking price is open to negotiation** for the right buyer.
