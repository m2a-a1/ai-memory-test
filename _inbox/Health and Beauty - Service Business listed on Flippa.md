## Listing Description

**QuickDoc Medical Limited – Business Overview**

QuickDoc.ca is an award-winning Canadian online healthcare platform providing fast, secure access to licensed medical professionals and prescription services nationwide. Founded two years ago, the business operates at the intersection of telehealth, digital compliance, and automation, offering patients streamlined access to consultations through a highly structured and safety-focused digital workflow. The platform is LegitScript certified, demonstrating adherence to strict healthcare compliance and pharmacy verification standards, and was named Winner of the 2026 Canadian Business Awards for Best Online Telehealth and Prescription Service. Positioned within Canada’s expanding digital healthcare market, QuickDoc.ca combines regulatory credibility, proprietary workflow automation, and scalable infrastructure to create a lean, technology-enabled medical service model with national reach.

**Key Financials (Trailing Twelve Months)**

- Annual Revenue: USD $2,173
- Annual Profit: USD $1,448

- Average Monthly Profit: USD $120
- Monthly Expenses: Approximately USD $83

The business operates with minimal overhead due to its digital-native structure and automated triage system. With limited fixed costs and no physical clinic requirements, profitability flows directly from consultation revenue, creating a capital-light model with strong operating leverage as patient volume scales.

**Business Model & Revenue Streams**

Revenue is primarily generated through per-visit consultation fees, with additional upside through potential revenue-share agreements with pharmacy partners. Patients complete structured digital health questionnaires that automatically route cases into Green, Amber, or Red categories. Green cases are auto-approved within predefined protocols, Amber cases are routed to clinician review, and Red cases are automatically declined. This triage system significantly reduces clinician time while maintaining strict patient safety and regulatory compliance. The proprietary back-office system integrates APIs to manage prescription routing, approvals, and operational oversight, enabling higher patient throughput with controlled clinical cost exposure.

**Technology & Compliance Infrastructure**

QuickDoc.ca operates on WordPress 6.8 with integrated analytics and payment systems including Square. The platform is supported by Google Analytics for performance tracking and includes custom-built technology that powers automated patient routing and internal workflow management. LegitScript certification provides regulatory credibility and enhances trust with patients, pharmacies, and advertising platforms. The business includes brand assets, trademarks, custom technology, unique design, and operational documentation necessary for continuity. Post-sale support is available to assist with regulatory understanding, system familiarisation, and partner transitions.

**Market Position & Traffic Performance**

The platform has generated 8,330 users and 12,978 page views, with approximately 1,081 monthly page views in Canada as the primary market. Organic search accounts for approximately 65% of traffic, demonstrating strong search intent around prescription-related keywords. The site ranks for over 1,120 keywords including high-intent terms related to antibiotics access and prescription medications in Canada. While the Authority Score currently stands at 8 with 29 referring domains and 34 backlinks, the SEO footprint establishes a base for structured content expansion and domain authority growth. The majority of traffic originates from Canada, with additional interest from the United States and other English-speaking markets, indicating cross-border informational demand.

**Customers & Brand Trust**

QuickDoc.ca is trusted by registered patients nationwide and benefits from award recognition and compliance certification, reinforcing credibility in a highly regulated industry. The email database includes 627 subscribers, providing a foundation for retention marketing, service reminders, and expansion into subscription-based offerings. The digital intake and automation model supports a discreet, efficient patient experience, appealing to individuals seeking convenience and fast prescription access.

**Growth Opportunities**

The Canadian telehealth sector continues to expand as patients increasingly adopt digital-first healthcare solutions. Growth levers include expanding service categories, introducing subscription-based prescription renewals, strengthening pharmacy revenue-share partnerships, and investing in SEO to capitalise on high-intent search queries already ranking organically. Paid acquisition remains largely untapped, presenting additional scaling channels. Further content optimisation and authority building could significantly increase organic traffic volume given the strong search intent in this niche. As patient volume increases, the automated triage system provides operating leverage without proportional cost increases, enhancing margin potential.

**Reason for Sale & Strategic Potential**

QuickDoc Medical Limited presents a compliant, award-recognised, and technology-enabled entry point into Canada’s digital healthcare market. The business is being offered with full operational assets, certification, and system infrastructure in place, allowing a buyer to focus on marketing expansion and service diversification rather than foundational development. With low fixed costs, regulatory credibility, and scalable automation, QuickDoc.ca represents a strategic acquisition opportunity within a high-growth telehealth environment.
