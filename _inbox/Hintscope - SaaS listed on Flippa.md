## Listing Description

Hintscope.com is an **AI sports prediction/reports and live score platform** covering major sports worldwide. Built from scratch on a modern tech stack and fully localized in 12 languages, Hintscope monetizes through a proven pay-per-use AI report system and active sportsbook affiliate partnerships—already generating revenue and affiliate sportsbook

**Key Platform Features AI Prediction Engine** 

- **Pay-Per-Use Reports:** Users purchase credits and spend them to generate AI match analyses
- **Comprehensive Output**: Outcome probabilities, xG models, BTTS deep analysis, exact score projections, player performance, and value bet detection
- **PayPal Integration**
- **Reports & Predictions Across 7 Sports**

**Live Sports Data & Coverage**

- **13+ Sports**: Football, basketball, tennis, and more live scores, fixtures, league standings, and full match statistics
- **Match Detail Pages**: H2H history, lineups, odds comparison, and prediction overlays per match
- **Value Bet:** Streak & Dropping Odds detection automatically
- **Sportsbook Reviews**: Detailed comparisons of betting platforms with active affiliate links and promo codes

**Betting Tools**

- **12 Free Calculators**
- **SEO Magnets**: These tools drive consistent organic traffic and act as top-of-funnel acquisition channels
- **Tipster-Ready**: User-submitted predictions infrastructure already built ready to evolve into a full tipster marketplace

**Technical Excellence**

- **12 Languages**
- **SEO Infrastructure**: Schema.org structured data, dynamic OG images, hreflang alternates, auto-generated sitemap
- **Multi-Layer Analytics**: Google Analytics 4, Umami, Microsoft Clarity

**Current Revenue Streams**

- **AI Credits:** Pay-per-use prediction reports
- **Affiliate Marketing**: **1xBet and Stake** sportsbook commissions
- **Editorial Content**: SEO-optimized articles and review pages generating organic traffic

**Growth Potential**

- **Subscription Tiers**: Monthly unlimited reports
- **Tipster Marketplace**: Community submission system already built - flip the switch and take a platform cut
- **Display Advertising**: High-traffic calculator and live score pages currently 100% unmonetized
- **API Licensing**: Sell prediction data to other operators
- **Telegram / Discord Bot**: Premium channels delivering daily AI picks

**Business Metrics**

- **Operating Costs**: Lean and predictable
	- Domain: ~$12/year
		- Cloud: $10/month
		- Sports data API: Free
		- AI model: per-report
		- **Time to Operate**: ~5–10 hours/week (mostly automated)

**Post-Sale Support**

- ✅ **3 Months Technical Support** from the original developer
- ✅ **Complete documentation**, deployment guides, and SOPs
- ✅ **Smooth handover** of all credentials, OAuth keys, affiliate accounts, and analytics access
