## Listing Description

This is a complete AI-powered sales system built for businesses using setter/closer models. It combines two complementary tools: LeadFollow AI, which re-engages inactive or unresponsive leads through structured follow-up to drive booked calls, and CloseSync AI, which converts setter conversations into tailored closing strategies with objection handling, scripts, and call guidance.

Together, the system improves the full pipeline from outreach to close, helping teams increase booked calls and close rates. Built using a modern stack (React, supabase, lovable, Stripe), both applications are fully functional and transferable.

The sale includes full ownership of both platforms projects, ICP and revenue playbooks (4 PDFs), a targeted 100-lead list, and early validation, making it ready for immediate commercialization with clear upside for a buyer who can execute. One sale pays for everything here.

ASKING PRICE REASONING:

The asking price reflects a bundled acquisition of two complementary AI sales platforms rather than a single standalone product. Together, LeadFollow AI and CloseSync AI cover both lead conversion and deal closing, while also including ICP research, revenue playbooks, validation, and a targeted lead list.

The system is designed for businesses operating in the $3k–$10k offer range, meaning a relatively small number of closed deals can realistically cover the acquisition cost. The pricing is positioned to allow a buyer to quickly commercialize and scale without spending months building the infrastructure from scratch.

Growth Opportunities / ROI Levers:

1: Outbound to Existing Lead Lists

The system is designed to be immediately deployed on existing lead databases. Many agencies and sales teams already have hundreds of inactive or unresponsive leads. Using LeadFollow AI’s structured follow-up sequences, buyers can re-engage these leads and recover previously lost opportunities without additional ad spend.

2: High-Ticket Offer Packaging

Both tools can be combined into a single “conversion system” offer for agencies and service businesses. Positioned around increasing booked calls and improving close rates, this can be sold as a premium service or internal tool for $10k+, allowing buyers to generate significant revenue from a small number of clients.

3: Cold Outreach Using Proven Scripts

The included playbooks contain validated outreach messaging and follow-up frameworks. A buyer can immediately begin contacting qualified prospects (LinkedIn, email, etc.) using the provided system, reducing time to first revenue.

4: Agency / Done-For-You Model

Instead of selling software subscriptions, buyers can offer this as a done-for-you service:

• Improve reply rates

• Recover ghosted leads

• Increase close rates

This allows for higher pricing and faster monetization.

5: Upsell Into Existing Sales Teams

Businesses already running setter/closer teams can integrate this system to improve performance. This creates a clear upsell opportunity for companies already investing in sales infrastructure.

Reason for selling:

Focusing on other projects and shifting time toward scaling ventures with existing traction. This system was built as a complete outbound and closing solution, but has not yet been fully commercialized. It’s a strong opportunity for a buyer who wants to take it to market and scale it.

Fully bootstrapped and built independently using modern AI-assisted development tools. No outside funding, debt, or investor obligations. This allows for a clean and straightforward transfer of ownership, with full control given to the buyer.
