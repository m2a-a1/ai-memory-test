##### About the Business

![](https://static.flippa.com/assets/icons/chevron-down-black-001d72ec84f37744a281555f70f57b3ca2aa3b7ad036e49133297265370531ad.svg)

**Startup description**

My tool is a free VIN decoder and Monroney window sticker generator for the US automotive market. Built on official NHTSA data (vPIC, Recalls, Safety Ratings, Complaints), the site covers 153 brands, 51,309 models, 133,529 model-year combinations, and 949,720 indexed URLs.

The signature feature is a free OEM-style Monroney window sticker generator — a tool most competitors charge for or hide behind registration walls. Combined with a fast VIN decoder and full vehicle data pages, my tool has earned a 4.9/5 rating across 1,324 user reviews.

**Why this asset is worth your attention**

- 9-year aged domain registered in 2017 — established search engine trust that cannot be replicated with money or time
- 949K indexed URLs across a programmatic SEO foundation built on official government data
- Proprietary Monroney window sticker engine — the strongest moat in the niche
- 97%+ net margin with operating costs under $15/month
- Dual verified monetization — Google AdSense and EpicVIN affiliate (30% rev share)
- Multi-engine traffic profile — not Google-dependent (Bing, Yandex, DuckDuckGo, AI crawlers all active).

**Current status — important context**

The site recently went through a major content optimization in early 2026. Low-value pages were removed, structure was tightened, and the indexing infrastructure was rebuilt for AI crawlers. The result:

- Bing, Yandex, DuckDuckGo and AI crawlers are actively re-crawling and ranking — traffic is growing fast
- Google is in re-evaluation phase — accumulating fresh signals on the rebuilt site (standard pattern after structural cleanup)

Translation: you are stepping in at the bottom of the curve, right before Google traffic returns to the cleaned-up index. The site is structurally healthier than ever.

**Traffic snapshot**

Bing alone delivers approximately 5,700 monthly clicks and 129K monthly impressions as of April 2026, with a steady upward trajectory since February. Daily clicks have grown from near-zero in early February to 110-125 per day on Bing alone. Verified traffic screenshots from Bing Webmaster Tools, Google Search Console, Yandex Metrika, and Microsoft Clarity are available in the data room.

**Revenue**

**Two active, verified revenue streams:**

- Google AdSense — display ads served across all VIN result pages and content sections. Active publisher account, ads.txt configured, no policy issues. Verified via AdSense API. $1,921 earned in the last 4 months.
- EpicVIN Affiliate — 30% revenue share on referred premium vehicle history reports. "Get Full Report" CTAs placed on every VIN result page. Verified via direct payouts. $201 earned so far.

Future revenue streams already architected into the system but not yet activated: paid premium window-sticker PDFs, dealer-grade bulk API access, embeddable widget licensing, and Telegram bot premium tier. Infrastructure is in place — activation is pure buyer-side upside.

**What's included in the sale**

- Domain with active SSL
- Complete codebase — PHP 8.2 backend (~32K lines across 105 files) and vanilla JS frontend
- Full MySQL database with 133K+ vehicle records and supporting tables
- Production Telegram bot built on Python aiogram (5 free checks/day per user)
- YouTube video generation pipeline — PHP + ffmpeg, ready to produce 150+ branded videos
- Embeddable VIN widget with JSONP API (rate-limited at 100 req/hour)
- AdSense and EpicVIN accounts (transferred per partner policy)
- Full SEO infrastructure — 30 sitemap files, 16 RSS feeds, llms.txt, ai.json, structured Schema.org
- IndexNow logs — 541,648 URLs already submitted to Bing and Yandex
- Outreach contact base and SEMrush competitor research as bonus material.

**Tech stack**

Deliberately simple and durable: PHP 8.2 + MySQL on Linux/Apache. Frontend is Vanilla JavaScript with no framework, no build pipeline, no compiled assets. Companion Python services handle the Telegram bot (aiogram) and video generation (Pillow + ffmpeg). Self-hosted on a standard VPS — fully transferable to any LAMP host within a single day.

No SaaS dependencies, no proprietary frameworks, no vendor lock-in. Any developer with general LAMP experience can take over without specialized onboarding.

**Operations**

The site is fully owner-operated and runs on a minimal-touch model — currently under 2 hours per week for routine operations. There are no employees, no contractors, no payroll, no HR overhead. The buyer can run it as passive income or invest 10-20 hours per week into growth opportunities. Both modes are valid.

**Why I'm selling**

I operate a portfolio of multiple web properties across different niches. the tool has reached the stage where it needs dedicated focus on SEO scaling, partnership outreach, video content production, and premium feature development to capitalize on its current growth trajectory. I'd rather transfer the asset to an operator who can give it that focus while I concentrate on other projects.

The site is structurally healthy, recently optimized, monetization is verified, and growth levers are clearly defined. This is a transition for the right operator, not a distress sale.

**Growth opportunities for the buyer**

- Activate the architectural premium tier — paid PDFs, dealer API, widget licensing, Telegram premium
- Geographic expansion — integrate EU, UK, or Canadian VIN data sources to multiply addressable market
- Video content scaling — generate 150+ branded YouTube videos using the existing PHP + ffmpeg pipeline
- Affiliate diversification — Carfax-alternatives, salvage-auction lookups, lien-check services
- Widget distribution — every third-party embed produces backlinks and referral traffic
- Conversion optimization — A/B test EpicVIN CTAs and AdSense placements across 949K pages.

**Why this is defensible**

Several layers of defensibility make competitive entry difficult:

- Aged domain (2017) — nine years of accumulated search engine trust cannot be replicated
- Proprietary Monroney engine — requires substantial automotive data engineering at scale across 153 brands
- SEO inventory — 949K indexed URLs cannot be created overnight
- Full AI-crawler stack — llms.txt, ai.json, structured schema; rare in the niche and increasingly important
- Direct NHTSA integration — official federal data source, no API costs, no data licensing risk.

**Pricing rationale**

Asking price reflects total asset value, not a revenue multiple. The bundle includes a 9-year-old domain, 949K indexed URLs, proprietary window-sticker engine, MySQL database with 133K vehicle records, embeddable widget with JSONP API, production Telegram bot, YouTube video pipeline, and SEO outreach base. Two verified revenue streams with 97%+ net margin. Site recently optimized — Bing, Yandex, DuckDuckGo growing fast, Google in re-indexing phase.

Replicating this asset from scratch would take 3-5 years and significant capital investment. Open to fair offers from serious operators.
