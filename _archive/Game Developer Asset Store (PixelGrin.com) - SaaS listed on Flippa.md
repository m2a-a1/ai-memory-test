**What is PixelGrin:**

PixelGrin is a specialized digital marketplace designed for the booming indie game industry. It functions similarly to the Unity Asset Store or Itch.io but is fully owned by you.

The platform connects two types of users:

- **Sellers (Artists):** They create their own branded storefronts, upload 2D sprites, UI kits, and textures, and manage their sales.
- **Buyers (Game Developers):** They browse the catalog, purchase licenses, and download files instantly to use in their games.

You, as the platform owner, provide the infrastructure and earn from the transactions that happen on your site.

**Why Should I Consider Buying This Marketplace?**

**How Do I Make Money with This Platform?**

The business model is flexible and automated:

- **Transaction Fees:** Earn a percentage of every sale made by artists on the platform.
- **Onboarding Fees:** The system has a built-in feature to charge artists a one-time fee (e.g., $20) to open a store.
- **Advertising:** With high traffic, you can sell banner space to game engines or software companies.
- **Featured Listings:** Charge sellers a premium to pin their assets to the top of the homepage.

**Why is a Marketplace Better Than Other Online Businesses?**

Marketplaces rely on **User Generated Content (UGC)**. Unlike a blog where you must write every article, or an e-commerce store where you must buy inventory, a marketplace grows itself. Artists create the products, upload the images, and write the descriptions for you. Your job is simply to manage the community and drive traffic.
