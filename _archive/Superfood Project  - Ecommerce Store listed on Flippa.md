## Listing Description

The Superfood Project Ltd is an early-stage UK children’s wellness brand focused on helping parents improve nutrition for picky eaters and texture-sensitive children through easy-to-use, great-tasting superfood powder blends.

Developed over the past three years with minimal paid advertising, the business has successfully validated both its product concept and audience demand within an engaged niche market, while leaving substantial room for future growth and scale.

The brand was created to solve a common parenting challenge: helping picky eaters and sensory-sensitive children access additional nutrients from real food in a simple, stress-free way.

The current product range includes four child-friendly superfood blends inspired by familiar flavours children already enjoy — strawberry, banana, apple, and blueberry. Each blend incorporates nutrient-dense ingredients including flaxseed, goji berry, golden berry, and broccoli, combining approachable taste profiles with functional nutritional benefits supporting areas such as digestion, immunity, energy, and heart health.

The business is strongly positioned within growing picky eater, children’s wellness, and sensory-sensitive parenting communities, with clear opportunities for expansion through:

- ecommerce scaling
- subscription growth
- influencer and creator partnerships
- Amazon marketplace expansion
- retail distribution

The category is well-suited to repeat purchases, as the products are designed for daily use, creating strong long-term subscription potential.

This business is ideally suited for a buyer looking to scale an already validated concept within the rapidly growing children’s wellness and functional nutrition market.

The sale includes the trademarked brand, ecommerce website, product formulations, social media accounts, customer email database, branding assets and supplier relationships.

The brand offers significant product expansion opportunities across adjacent children’s wellness categories, including bedtime blends, lunchbox snacks, functional treats, and other family-focused nutrition products, creating clear potential for line extension and increased customer lifetime value.
