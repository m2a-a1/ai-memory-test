## Listing Description

**What is dumbphone.fr?**

Dumbphone.fr is a French-language content site dedicated to living without a smartphone. It covers dumbphone and feature phone reviews (Light Phone 3, Nokia 225 4G…), practical guides (banking, working, navigating without a smartphone), product reviews for smartphone-replacing accessories (GPS devices, security keys, music players, kids’ watches), and reading recommendations on digital attention and screen addiction.

The site publishes 3–5 articles per week and targets a fast-growing French audience interested in digital minimalism and smartphone alternatives.

**Traffic**

5,952 unique visitors and 26,099 page views in the last 30 days — up +24% and +33% respectively. Traffic is overwhelmingly organic: Google alone drives 3.1K visitors/month, with additional search traffic from DuckDuckGo, Brave, Ecosia, and Qwant. The site has also been referenced by Le Monde (lemonde.fr), France’s leading newspaper.

84% of visitors are based in France, confirming the site’s dominance of the French-speaking dumbphone niche. Bounce rate sits at a healthy 46%.

**Content & SEO**

- 709 organic keywords ranked on Google
- 133 backlinks from 68 referring domains
- Authority Score: 9
- Top keywords: “dumbphone”, “dumb phone”, “dumbphone whatsapp”

All content is original, well-researched, and written in French — a language with very little competition in this niche

The site occupies a unique position: it is the only dedicated French-language resource for the dumbphone/feature phone market. English-language competitors exist, but none target the francophone audience specifically.

**Monetization**

Revenue comes from Amazon Associates affiliate links embedded in product reviews and buying guides. Current annual revenue: ~$698 with a 97% profit margin (only expense: $2/month hosting).

The affiliate model is straightforward and requires no inventory, no customer support, and no fulfillment.

**Growth Opportunities**

Display ads: The site’s content-heavy format and 26K+ monthly page views make it a strong candidate for Mediavine or Raptive once traffic thresholds are met

Expanded affiliate programs: Direct partnerships with dumbphone brands (Light Phone, Punkt, Nokia) could yield higher commissions than Amazon

Newsletter monetization: The existing email subscriber list can be monetized through sponsored content or paid recommendations

Product guides / ebooks: Paid downloadable guides (“How to switch from smartphone to dumbphone”) targeting the growing digital detox audience

YouTube / video content: Video reviews and tutorials to capture search traffic on YouTube FR
