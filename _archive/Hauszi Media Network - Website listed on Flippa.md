## Listing Description

**Hauszi Media Network** is a Pinterest-driven home decor content business built as a multi-site, multi-language brand targeting global audiences.

Details - [Info](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1cNzlLEvzhWR4ob_tf6vJCfzrxTHzu2wH/view?usp=sharing) and [earnings](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1S6SntfLYhbLyDNXVcLYBj-s588VsWQZW/view?usp=sharing)  
             

What started as a learning project has evolved into a scalable digital media network with **6 niche websites**, strong Pinterest distribution, and consistent monthly revenue.

? Brand Overview

Hauszi is positioned as a **home decor inspiration brand**, publishing visually engaging content designed to perform on Pinterest and attract high-intent users.

The network includes:

- *[hauszi.de](https://hauszi.de/)(primary site – Germany, top performer)*
- *[hauszi.nl](https://hauszi.nl/)(Netherlands)*
- *[hauszi.fr](https://hauszi.fr/)(France)*
- *[hauszi.es](https://hauszi.es/)(Spain)*
- *[hauszi.it](https://hauszi.it/)(Italy)*
- *[hauszi.net](https://hauszi.net/)(global/English)*

This multi-language setup allows expansion into multiple high-value markets with the same proven content model.

? Traffic & Reach

- **~22M monthly Pinterest impressions (combined)**
- Primary traffic source: Pinterest
- Strong presence in **Germany and European markets**
- Content optimized for viral distribution (image-heavy, Pinterest-first strategy)

The traffic model is **repeatable and scalable**, making it easy to expand further into new regions.

? Revenue

- **~$1,500/month average revenue**
- Monetized primarily through display ads
- Revenue generated across multiple sites

There is **significant upside** as current monetization is minimal compared to traffic potential.

⚙️ Operations

- Fully remote, location-independent business
- Streamlined content + publishing workflow
- Pinterest strategy already tested and working
- Low time requirement to maintain (can be optimized further)

? Growth Opportunities

This is where the real value lies:

- Add **affiliate marketing (home decor products)**
- Launch **digital products (printables, guides, design kits)**
- Expand into **eCommerce (dropshipping / branded store)**
- Increase content output using existing system
- Scale Pinterest accounts further
- Expand into additional languages/regions

The current setup proves the concept—**a buyer can scale this aggressively**.

? Why this is valuable

- Proven Pinterest traffic engine
- Multi-site brand structure (not a single site risk)
- Strong niche (home decor = high monetization potential)
- International audience
- Under-monetized → clear upside

With multiple ventures and new projects planned, I’m looking to pass this on to someone who can fully focus on scaling it and unlocking its full potential.

Hauszi is not just a group of websites—it’s a **scalable content brand with a proven traffic system**.
